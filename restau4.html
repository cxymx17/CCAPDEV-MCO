Lugang Cafe

Lugang Café serves authentic Taiwanese cuisine. It is best known for its xiao long bao – dumplings covered in thin, delicate wrappers. Other dishes that have earned rave reviews from diners include their wonton soup, roast duck, and that Three-Cup Chicken house specialty.
